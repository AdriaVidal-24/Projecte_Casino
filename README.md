## GAMES
### BLACKJACK
Blackjack is a popular card game played in casinos worldwide, where the objective is to beat the dealer by obtaining a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.

### ROULETTE
Roulette is a game in which a small ball is spun around on a moving wheel, and people try to win money by guessing which hole the ball will fall into.
### COINSLOT
Slot machines are gambling machines where a certain amount of money is wagered to win a cash prize.
### COIN FLIP
CoinFlip games are pure chance-based gambling games where players bet on the outcome of a virtual or live coin toss. The game follows a 50/50 probability, meaning there are only two possible results: heads or tails.
